*   *foro:* I pierce

^ *foro* is from proto-indo-european word  *bʰerH- meaning to pierce or strike

^ related to:

^ *   Lithuanian bárti (“to accuse, scold”)

^ *   Old Church Slavonic брати (brati, “to fight”)

^ *   Icelandic berja (“to beat”)

# Modified Atmosphere Packaging

![left](pictures/gas.jpg)

^ popularized in the 90s

^ fruits and vegetables *respirate:* consume oxygen and leak carbon dioxide.

^ MAP is about controlling the gases in the bag.

---

![](pictures/maplettuce.jpg)

^ new tech: laser microperforation,

^ Selectively permeable films on passive MAP foods allows some gases to escape and traps others inside

---

![](pictures/packs.jpg)

^ oxygen can be introduced at the rate it's consumed

^ carbon dioxide can be vented at the rate it's created

^ equilibrium modified atmosphere, or EMA
